Easy button recipe, using all pre-prepared stuff.

🌡️🔪🥣⏲️✅➡️

🥣 4x 400ml tins cut green beans  
✅ 4x 400ml tins Sainsbury's cream of mushroom soup  
✅ 2x veggie bullion pots  
🔪 750g smoked gammon joint, diced

## Bake
30 min at 175c fan.
